How It Works

Three simple steps to better self-understanding.

1

Choose Your Metrics

Pick from built-in metrics (mood, sleep, coffee, steps, screen time) or create your own custom ones. Tailor Loom to what matters to you.

2

Log Daily

Spend 30 seconds each day logging your metrics. Add notes for context. Build your streak and watch the data accumulate.

3

Discover Patterns

After 7+ days, Loom reveals correlations, trends, and anomalies in your data. See how your habits connect.
